Electron-hole and exciton systems

I started learning about laser-excited dynamics in semiconductors during the last year of my PhD. One line of research concerns an experimentally observed ring-shaped luminescence pattern. (I started work on this with Phil Platzman of Bell Labs when I was in New Jersey, and later continued by myself in Utrecht.) A second direction involves more basic questions, involving coherence and condensation in driven non-equilibrium systems.
