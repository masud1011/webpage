Online journal club on Many-Body Open Quantum Systems

Mainly covering papers on Lindblad many-body systems.

We meet via zoom, so not restricted to any particular institution or country. However, the timing is more suitable for people located in Europe and Asia.

Mondays 10:30am - noon CET (Germany/Italy), which means 9:30-11:00 Ireland/Portugal.
In summer, this means starting at 2pm India, 4:30pm China.   (In winter: 3pm India, 5:30pm China.)

In each session, one person presents a paper, usually sharing their own notes or slides about the paper over zoom.

If you are interested in participating or just listening in, please contact me by email:
