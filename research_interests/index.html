RESEARCH INTERESTS





  • Strongly Correlated Electron Systems.
    • Formal models: Hubbard, Heisenberg, Ising, quantum rotor.
    • Transport and optical phenomena in quantum wells and dots.
    • Kondo physics, heavy fermion materials. High-Tc cuprate superconductors.
    • 1D: Luttinger liquid physics, nanotubes & quantum wires, bosonization techniques.
    • Vortex physics in superconductors.
    • More generally: quantum phase transitions, superconductivity and magnetism.

  • Two-Dimensional Systems.
    • Kosterlitz-Thouless transitions.
    • Wigner crystals in semiconductors and on cryogenic liquids.
    • Metal-insulator transition in semiconductor heterojunctions.
    • Quantum Hall physics; in heterojunctions, and in rotating Bose condensates.
    • Vortex lattices, in superconductors and trapped atomic clouds.

  • Bose-Einstein Condensation.
    • Formalism for weakly interacting Bose gas
      (Field theoretical, variational, RG, and effective-action approaches).
    • BEC of high-density excitons in coupled quantum wells.
    • Interaction of condensates with photon field.
    • Interference of condensates; states with multiple macroscopically occupied modes.

  • Trapped Atomic Gases.
    • Trapping in Optical Lattice. Superfluid-insulator transition; Bose-Hubbard model.
    • Trapped fermionic atoms - quantum degeneracy effects; pairing & superfluidity; sympathetic cooling.
    • Light scattering off trapped BEC, Super-radiance.
    • Vortices in rotating clouds.
    • Multi-component (spinor) condensates - skyrmions, interpenetrating vortex lattices.

  • Non-equilibrium Phenomena.
    • In general: Applying Keldysh or Boltzmann-equation formalism to non-equilibrium phenomena.
    • Non-equilibrium effects of laser radiation on excitonic matter and trapped atomic condensates .
